Adobo with potatoes

ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Potatoes, 4pcs
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic, 5 cloves
  • Onion, 1pc
  • Black pepper, crushed or whole, depends on you
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • MSG *wink*
  • Oil, ofc

    • procedure

    1. SautÃ© garlic and onions along with the chickens until it became little golden brown
    2. Add soy sauce, vinegar, water and potatoes
    3. Add peppers and msg
    4. Add water and sugar to balance the taste
    5. Let it boil with low heat until the chicken and potatoes is tender
    6. If the sauce is a bit thick or goes to your preference, it is ready to serve
