Tomato Saba Curry (by Yagai No Moriko-san)

ingredients

  • Onion
  • Potatoes/Sweet potatoes, 4pcs
  • Curry powder
  • Saba, canned
  • Tomatoes, canned if available
  • Black pepper, optional
  • Oil

    • procedure

    1. Put oil in the pan
    2. Saute onion
    3. Add the Sweet potatoes or just potatoes , depending on your preference
    4. Add curry powder,idk measurement
    5. Add the Saba/Mackarel
    6. And put the diced/canned tomatoes and let simmer until cooked
