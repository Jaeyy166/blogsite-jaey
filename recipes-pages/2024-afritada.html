Chicken Afritada

ingredients

  • Chicken(ofc)
  • Potatoes
  • Tomatoes
  • Black pepper
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion

procedure

  1. sautee garlic, onion and tomatoes first
  2. then stir fry the chicken along with the garlic, onion and tomatoes
  3. when chicken becomes golden brown, add soy sauce and water
  4. add salt and pepper to taste
  5. add potatoes and let it simmer until cooked
