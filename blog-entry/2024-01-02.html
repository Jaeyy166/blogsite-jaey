On death

dad is already gone this day. im currently typing this
january 30.
he wants chicken fillet ala king that im eating the night
before he died, so i did feed him a spoon of chicken fillet
and rice. he did complain that its oily lol.
later, after mom go out it buy some biscuits for dad,
dad ask me to massage his legs cos he says its aching,
so i did, im really sad inside because dad turn into this state
in span of a year and half.

we sleep. it was like 2 or 3 am idk i dont remember the time,
but mom waked me up cos dad is having a fever, me and mom take
care of dad. i wipe cold towel on him while i ask mom to call
doctors. doctor said if dad dont get tube on his lungs,
he will stop breathing.
mom called tito to come at the hospital too. they decided to
not put tube. as im tending dad while hes having high fever
his eyes were just looking lost occasionally looking at me,
i looked at him too, couldnt look at him because of how much
he is struggling that night, no, all the time hes alive.
tito looked out for dad while me and mom sleep at the ground floor
we came back and the pulmo department doing some injections on him
and he suddenly stops breathing, i pat his leg to wake him up and
shake him, but hes not responding anymore, it was daybreak.
we cried, trying to hold back tears from falling from my eyes.
trying hard to be tough in front of mom, whos crying hard.
"so thats it, its just after new years", i said inside my head.
life moves on, other people dont care or dont know that you lost
someone this day.

dad, wherever you are now, i hope youre already resting,
finally able to be at peace, free from pain and suffering.
pahinga ka na dyan pa, mahal ka namin.
